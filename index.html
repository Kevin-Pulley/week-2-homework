About Me


Hello! My name is Kevin Pulley. I was born in Bowling Green, KY in 1984. I moved to Nashville when I was 13 and have been here since. I graduated from Beech High in 2003, and worked with my dad at his car lot until I was 20. Since then I've worked at a company called Symspire where we do low voltage wiring on new residental houses, and have been there 15 years.
